About our school

Oslomet or Oslo metropolitan university is an international university located in the center of Oslo. The university was previously a college, but was converted into a university in 2018. The school is one of the largest in Norway, with over 20,000 students, and has many different fields of study to offer. Here you will find everything from computer science to teacher training, and by 2023 will have over 100 bachelor's and master's studies.
